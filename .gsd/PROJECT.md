# WrenchLog

**Ad-free vehicle maintenance tracker for iOS. Never miss an oil change again.**

## What It Is

WrenchLog helps car owners track maintenance history, set service reminders, log fuel costs, and keep their vehicles healthy. Multiple vehicles, full service history, smart reminders based on mileage and time.

## Current State

Research phase — M001 in progress.

## Stack

- SwiftUI + SwiftData
- StoreKit 2 (native IAP)
- iOS 17+
- No third-party dependencies

## Core Principles

- **No ads ever** — premium model only
- **Privacy first** — all data on-device, no tracking, no data selling
- **Multi-vehicle** — car, motorcycle, truck — unlimited in free tier
- **US + EU market focus** — miles/km, USD/EUR, US service intervals

## Target Audience

- Car enthusiasts who do their own maintenance
- Multi-car families tracking multiple vehicles
- People who want service history for resale value
- Fleet owners (small business)
